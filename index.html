My roots and lineage have been my driving force, informing my life's work and the causes that I dedicate myself to. I grew up surrounded by women, and it has been their influence, trauma, and healing process that inspired me to become a Rape Crisis Counselor as an adult. My work with survivors of sexual violence has been particularly impactful, and it drove me to the intersection of tech and social justice.

How can technology truly embody access, when services survivors need are behind phones they do not have? How (and where) can technology contribute to a world free of sexual violence?

Pivoting into the tech world has allowed me to explore solutions to these challenges that can hopefully create a safer and more equitable world for everyone. I am committed to leveraging technology as a tool for social change that ensures that survivors and everyone alike have access to the resources and support they need to heal and thrive.
